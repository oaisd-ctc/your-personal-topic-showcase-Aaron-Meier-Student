Easy Chili Recipe

Learn how to make chili with only 5 ingredients!

How to make easy chili

Chili Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces of tomato sauce
  • 1 can or 15 ounces of drained kidney beans
  • 1 can of 16 ounces of undrained chili beans
  • 2 tablespeeds of chili powder

Checkout Walmart!

Cooking Directions

  1. Step 1

    Heat a pan of the stove at a medium-high temperature. Cook the lean ground beef in the pan for 5-7 minutes while occasionally stiring until browned.

  2. Step 2

    Add the rest of the ingredients and stir occasionally until boiling. Reduce heat to simmer and let cook for about 15 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Enjoy your freshly made easy chili!

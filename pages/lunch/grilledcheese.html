Grilled Cheese Recipe

Learn how to make Grilled Cheese Sandwiches!

How to make grilled cheese

Sandwich Ingredients

  • 2 slices of any bread, white recommended
  • 1 ½ tablespoons of butter
  • 1 slice of any cheese, chedder recommended

Need to order Ingredients or Cooking supplies?

Checkout Walmart!

Cooking Directions

  1. Step 1

    Butter both the slices of bread and with the butter sides facing outward, put the cheese inbetween.

  2. Step 2

    Heat a pan of the stove at a medium-high temperature. Place your sandwich on the pan and flip occasionally until cheese is completely melted.

  3. Step 3

    Enjoy your freshly made grilled cheese sandwich!

Pancakes Recipe

Learn how to make Delicious hot cakes!

How to make pancakes

Batter Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 ¼ cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 egg

Need to order Ingredients or Cooking supplies?

Checkout Walmart!

Cooking Directions

  1. Step 1

    Mix all the Ingredients together in a large bowl until you have a smooth paste.

  2. Step 2

    Heat a pan of the stove at a medium-high temperature. Pour ¼ cup of batter to make each pancake on the pan. Cook for about 2-3 minutes for each pancake then flip and repeat. You can also cook until brown on both sides.

  3. Step 3

    Enjoy your freshly made pancakes!
